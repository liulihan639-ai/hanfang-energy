Our Journey

2020

Company Founded

Hanfang Energy was officially founded in Heze, Shandong, beginning ESS R&D and product development.

2021

First Products Launched

First wall-mounted and stackable ESS products launched, certified with IEC62619 and UN38.3.

2022

ISO Triple Certification

Achieved ISO9001, ISO14001, ISO45001 triple certification, upgrading quality management system.

2023

C&I Product Line Expansion

Launched liquid/air-cooled ESS cabinets and C&I systems, covering residential to C&I scenarios.

PICC Insurance · Overseas Expansion

All products covered by PICC insurance, successfully entered Southeast Asia market with Malaysia TCT project.

Large-Scale ESS Launch
